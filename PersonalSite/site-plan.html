Overview

Purpose

If you're like me you are constantly trying to find ways to optimize your health in all areas. Where do you go to find this information? You can google and sift through the mounds of opinion mixed in with fact, or you can try my site “OptimalHealth.com”. At OptimalHealth.com you can find exactly what you are searching for, navigating Physical, Mental/Emotional, and Spiritual health sub categories in order to find the Optimization you are looking for. Backed by evidentiary studies linked to each topic, you can find the data driven science of how to optimize your health. Articles by industry professionals and recognized health coaches to provide you with the most thorough methods for living an optimal life.

Audience

The audience is characterized predomininatly by people looking to optimize their health. This is a large target market consisting of those already interested in health and fitness, and also those seeeking to make various life changes.
